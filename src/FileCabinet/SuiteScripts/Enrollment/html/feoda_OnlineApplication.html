Terms & Conditions

Parent and Student Agreement and Declaration

I understand and agree to the following if accepted to be a part of Weddington Academy:
1. Supportive of all Policies and Guidelines of the Academy including those relating to uniform, and agree to support the school’s Pastoral Care Policy
2. To participate in the Religious Education Program of the Academy which will include, classes and Masses, Academy Social Justice Program, Academy religious celebrations, parish activities and retreats. Agree that camps, activities and retreats, where relevant to the particular year group, form an integral part of the Academy curriculum and are compulsory for all students and hereby give permission for the attending student to participate in such activities.
3. Payment of educational fees to Weddington Academy each year the student is enrolled at the Academy. If financial hardship occurs, the Academy will be contacted via the Academy Business Manager, to discuss alternative payment arrangements. Ten school weeks’ notice of withdrawal of a student from the Academy is required. Upon withdrawal, families will remain liable for a weekly pro-rata portion of all fees up to the withdrawal date or a date ten weeks after notice is given whichever is the latest. Notice of withdrawal must be made in writing to the Principal.
4. Give permission for the school authorities to authorise any medical steps which may become necessary as the result of any accident occurring at the Academy or at functions/excursions organised by the Academy if contact cannot be made with nominated emergency contacts before any such treatment is deemed necessary by proper medical authorities.
5. Give permission for Weddington Academy to contact the current school attended by the student and release personal and/or sensitive information for funding and educational purposes, and understand that this information will enable Weddington Academy to monitor and guide the students’ progress more effectively.
6. The information collected in this application to enrol at Weddington Academy will be used to enable the Academy to discharge its duty of care.
7. This application is not considered complete (and accepted), until all documentation requested has been received correctly by the Enrolments Office.
8. For this enrolment process to be finalised, an ‘Acceptance of Offer of Enrolment Agreement’ will need to be signed should this application be successful.
Parents and students should read the information and policies set out by the Academy prior to agreeing to the terms and conditions.
